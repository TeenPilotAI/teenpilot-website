🏫 For Schools

One integration.
Every student.

TeenPilot connects to Canvas (SchoolBridge) so students onboard automatically. Each school gets their own branded subdomain, bell times and term configuration.

  • Canvas / SchoolBridge API integration — one approval, all students
  • School-specific subdomain (westlake.teenpilot.co.nz)
  • Custom bell times, cycle length and term dates
  • Site licence pricing — one cost for the whole school
  • Student, parent and teacher accounts under one roof
  • NZ curriculum and NCEA-aware from day one
