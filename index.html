Built for everyone

One app.
Three views.

TeenPilot works differently for students, parents and schools — each getting exactly what they need.

🎒
For Students

Your personal AI Co-Pilot that knows your timetable, keeps track of what's due, and helps you stay on top of school, sport and life.

  • Daily flight plan — know what's on before you leave the house
  • Upload worksheets and get instant AI help
  • Study plans and practice questions for every exam
  • Voice chat with Co-Pilot on the way to school
  • Squad chats with your team or study group
👨‍👩‍👦
For Parents

A calm weekly briefing — not surveillance. Know what's coming up, spot overdue work early, and ask Co-Pilot questions about your child's week.

  • Weekly AI summary — assignments, exams, sport, workload
  • Overdue alert before it becomes a problem
  • Full week schedule day by day
  • Ask Co-Pilot anything about your child's week
  • Exam tracker with topics and urgency
🏫
For Schools

TeenPilot integrates with Canvas (SchoolBridge) to pull timetables and assignments automatically. Students onboard in seconds.

  • Canvas / SchoolBridge API integration
  • School-specific timetable and bell times
  • NZ school terms and public holidays built in
  • School-branded subdomain
  • Site licence pricing for whole school rollout
